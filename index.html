About Me

Hi! My name is Raul Medeiros, and I am a Software Engineering PhD student at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU), Spain. I have a BSc in Computer Science from the same university and a MSc in Software Engineering from the University of Murcia. After a brief period of time working as a software developer, I decided to pursue a PhD in Software Engineering.

My recent research on Software Product Line Engineering led me to publish some research papers during the last years. However, I've always been attracted from the industrial side of software engineering, where you can tackle more technical problems that are closer to the real world. So, when I finish my PhD, I would like to work in the industry as a software engineer or architect.

Pre-doctoral Researcher

University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU) - Onekin Research Group• September 2018 - Present

Development of my PhD Thesis. Currently, the main topic of my dissertation is Software Product Line Engineering (SPLE). Particularly, I'm working in three different aspects of SPLE:

  1. SPL Onboarding (i.e. the process of incorporating a new developer into an SPL organization)
  2. Visualization or Visual Analytics (i.e. the science of analytical reasoning facilitated by interactive visual interfaces)
  3. Continuous Deployment (i.e. An extension of Agile practices whereby the user feedback is extended beyond development to learn from their USAGE)
