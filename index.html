About Me

Hi! My name is Raul Medeiros, and I am a Software Engineering PhD student working for the Onekin Research Group at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU), Spain. I have a BSc in Computer Science from the same university and a MSc in Software Engineering from the University of Murcia. After a brief period of time working as a software developer, I decided to pursue a PhD in Software Engineering.

My recent research in Software Product Line Engineering has led me to publish several research papers in the last few years. However, I have always been drawn to the practical side of software engineering, where I can tackle more complex, real-world problems. After completing my PhD, I am interested in working in the industry as a software engineer or architect.

Talking about my hobbies, I am a lover of adventure and technology. In my free time, you can find me out on the trails riding my mountain bike, getting lost in the worlds of epic fantasy books like those written by Brandon Sanderson and Patrick Rothfuss, delving into the latest developments in blockchain technology, and competing in my favorite video games. I am constantly seeking new challenges and experiences, and I bring that same energy and curiosity to everything I do.
